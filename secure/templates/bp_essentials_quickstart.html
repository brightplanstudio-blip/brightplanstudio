Quick-Start Checklist

Today (30 minutes)

  1. Set 3 outcomes for this week; block time in calendar.
  2. Create KPI tree: North-Star + two drivers.
  3. Adopt daily 10-minute review (AM + shutdown).

This Week

  1. Measure: completion %, focus hours, interruptions/day.
  2. Run Friday 20-minute review; archive a dashboard snapshot.

This Month

  1. Cut lead time by 20% by removing one bottleneck.
  2. Lift completion to 80% with weekly capacity checks.

Reminder: Consistency beats intensity. Protect focus blocks and limit priorities to three.
